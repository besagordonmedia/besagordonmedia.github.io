Turning audiences into advocates through authentic storytelling.

Besa Gordon Media (BGM) is a storytelling and community engagement agency that helps organizations build trust, increase visibility, and create meaningful connections.

The agency

Founded by media personality and longtime storyteller Besa Gordon, BGM brings more than a decade of experience across television, radio, digital media, live events, partnership development, and community engagement.

Through strategic content creation, video production, social media campaigns, creator partnerships, event hosting, and community-focused initiatives, BGM helps organizations share their stories in ways that resonate with the people they serve.

The founder

Besa is best known as the former host of Back2Besa on FOX 13 Seattle, where she spent years highlighting businesses, nonprofits, cultural institutions, community leaders, and events throughout the Pacific Northwest.

That experience shaped BGM's approach: finding the stories that matter and telling them in a way that feels relatable, engaging, and impactful.

Who we work with

BGM has worked with professional sports organizations, arts and cultural institutions, nonprofits, local businesses, government agencies, and community leaders — creating campaigns and experiences that strengthen relationships and foster genuine community connection.
